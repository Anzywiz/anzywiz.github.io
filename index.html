What "color-variant level" means
Standard product-level scraper
1 row per product
A blazer is 1 row. No visibility into its 5 colors, no per-color image, no way to know if colors differ in price.
This dataset — color-variant level
1 row per product × color
Same blazer → 5 rows (Pink Rose Gem, Sweet Pea, Skyride, Icy Rose, Deep Black). Each has its own image, deep link, and price — because Macy's sometimes prices colors differently.

For example, the Forever Grown Diamonds ring in the sample data appears as two rows — "Sterling Silver" and "Gold-Plated Sterling Silver" — each priced at $199 (full price $1,000, 80% off). A product-level scraper would capture only one color and miss the other's image and URL entirely.
