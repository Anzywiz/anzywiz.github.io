What "color-variant level" means
Standard product-level scraper
1 row per product
A blazer is 1 row. No visibility into its 5 colors, no per-color image, no way to know if colors are priced differently.
This dataset — color-variant level
1 row per product × color
Same blazer → 5 rows: Pink Rose Gem, Sweet Pea, Skyride, Icy Rose, Deep Black. Each has its own image, deep link, and price. A diamond ring priced $1,000 full / $199 sale appears as separate rows per metal color.

The product_code field uses the format product_id::color_id — letting you group by parent product or filter by specific color variant. Average 1.4 colors per product; up to 35 for highly varied items.
