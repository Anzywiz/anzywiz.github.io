02 — Granularity

Product-level, not variant-stacked

This dataset operates at the product level: each row represents a single watch SKU as identified by its website product code (e.g. CDW2181.FC8360). Color, size, and material information is captured as structured text attributes within the row — not as separate rows per variant.

Note on pricing: tagheuer.com displays a single price per product — no promotional or sale price exists on the site. price and full_price are therefore identical in every row and both are delivered. Store-level pricing is not available on tagheuer.com.
